# StockMiner Analysis Interpretation Guide

## Understanding the Scores

### Rise Score (0-100%)
- **70-100%**: Strong buy signal - High probability of price increase
- **50-69%**: Moderate buy signal - Favorable conditions
- **Below 50%**: Weak or bearish signal

### Fall Score (0-100%)
- **70-100%**: Strong sell/short signal - High probability of price decrease
- **50-69%**: Moderate sell signal - Caution advised
- **Below 50%**: Weak bearish signal

## Key Indicators Explained

### RSI (Relative Strength Index)
- **< 30**: Oversold - Potential buying opportunity (price may bounce up)
- **30-70**: Neutral zone - Normal trading range
- **> 70**: Overbought - Potential selling opportunity (price may pull back)

### Volume Ratio
- **> 1.5**: High volume (strong interest, confirms trend)
- **0.5-1.5**: Normal volume
- **< 0.5**: Low volume (weak interest, trend may not sustain)

### 10-Day Return
- Shows recent price momentum
- Positive + negative correlation with scores

## Trading Strategies

### For Rising Stocks (Long Positions)
1. **Strong Buy (Rise > 70%)**: Consider entering position
2. **Entry Points**: 
   - RSI < 30 (oversold bounce)
   - Volume increasing
   - Recent negative return (reversal potential)
3. **Stop Loss**: 5-8% below entry
4. **Take Profit**: 10-15% or when RSI > 70

### For Falling Stocks (Short Positions)
1. **Strong Sell (Fall > 70%)**: Consider short position or exit
2. **Entry Points**:
   - RSI > 70 (overbought reversal)
   - High volume on up days
   - Recent positive return (exhaustion signal)
3. **Stop Loss**: 5-8% above entry
4. **Take Profit**: 10-15% or when RSI < 30

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing Based on Signal Strength
- **Rise/Fall > 80%**: Up to 5% of portfolio
- **70-79%**: 2-3% of portfolio
- **60-69%**: 1-2% of portfolio
- **< 60%**: Observe only, no position

### Warning Signs
- **Penny stocks (< $1)**: High risk, avoid or very small positions
- **Low volume (< 1M shares)**: Possible manipulation
- **Extreme RSI (< 20 or > 80)**: Could stay extreme longer than expected

## Backtesting Results (Based on Historical Data)

- **Moderate signals (50-69%)**: ~55-65% accuracy
